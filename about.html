The Chrome Hearts Legacy

Founded in 1988 by Richard Stark, Chrome Hearts began as a vision to create the perfect leather riding jacket. What emerged was a revolutionary brand that defied conventional luxury, blending rock 'n' roll rebellion with unparalleled craftsmanship in sterling silver, leather, and fine woods.

From its humble beginnings in a Los Angeles garage, Chrome Hearts rapidly gained a cult following among musicians, celebrities, and fashion connoisseurs. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted, embodying a philosophy of uncompromising quality and unique design that stands the test of time.

What truly isolates Chrome Hearts from the competition is its dedication to authenticity and exclusivity. We don't follow trends; we set them. Our designs are not mass-produced; they are individual works of art, imbued with a spirit of individuality and a rebellious edge that resonates with those who forge their own path. This commitment to genuine artistry and a distinct identity ensures that every Chrome Hearts item is not just a product, but a statement.

We invite you to delve deeper into the world where luxury meets rebellion, and craftsmanship reigns supreme.
